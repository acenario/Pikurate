Don't Experience the Internet Alone

Together, we can find information hidden in our online universe.

The Problem

Finding the right information online is not easy.

When we want to learn more about a topic, we embark on an online search by ourselves to find the best articles, videos, and more. Each of us spends hours scouring the web for useful information just to close all of our tabs or windows after our search.

This is the problem.

Why are we searching alone, when many people are searching for the same information at the same time? Why don’t we save our most useful links on a topic to a centralized platform?

The Solution: Pikurate

What if you could consume information in a better way?

Pikurate is redefining how people find information online because we believe in collaborating on our most pressing searches. Our platform allows users to save, share, and search for really useful content. People can see what they may have missed during their research and learn something they didn't expect to. With Pikurate, people will spend less time uselessly browsing and more time consuming information together.
